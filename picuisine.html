🍳 What is Picuisine?
Picuisine is an innovative AI cooking assistant that uses your mobile camera to identify ingredients in real-time. Simply point your camera at any food items, and our AI will instantly suggest delicious recipes, cooking techniques, and creative dishes you can make with what you have on hand.
📱 How It Works
  1. 📸Point & Scan: Open the app and point your camera at ingredients, leftovers, or any food items
  2. 🤖AI Recognition: Our advanced AI instantly identifies and analyzes the ingredients in your viewfinder
  3. 🍽️Get Suggestions: Receive personalized recipe recommendations, cooking tips, and creative dish ideas
  4. 👨‍🍳Start Cooking: Follow step-by-step instructions or adapt recipes to your taste
