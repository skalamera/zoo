🍳 What is Picuisine?
Picuisine is an innovative AI cooking assistant that uses your mobile camera to identify ingredients through video recording. Record a short video of your food items, and our AI will analyze the footage to suggest delicious recipes, cooking techniques, and creative dishes you can make with what you have available.
📱 How It Works
  1. 📸Start Camera: Open the camera viewfinder and point it at your ingredients
  2. 🔴Record Video: Tap "Start Recording" and move your camera around the food items for a few seconds
  3. ⏹️Stop & Analyze: Tap "Stop & Analyze" to send your video to our AI for ingredient identification
  4. 🍽️Get Suggestions: View recipe recommendations based on your ingredients
  5. 👨‍🍳Cook: Tap any suggestion for detailed step-by-step cooking instructions
